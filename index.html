Why Programming?


Computer programming is important today because so much of our world is automated. Humans need to be able to control the interaction between people and machines.

Programming is everywhere in the modern world and meets you in the street, your workplace,
and the local grocery store and supermarkets.
You interact with bar-code scanners regularly, and you almost certainly use lots of code
while working, whether you're using a word processor to
